The History Of Football

ORIGIN

Football was invented by Walter Camp and is now a world wide sport!

The first game was played in 1869 between Rutgers University and College of New Jersey.

THE GAME

Rules:

  • Each team will have 11 players on the field.
  • The offense is the unit that's responsible for trying to score points for their team and they will do this by running or passing the football down the field. This includes Five offensive linemen (one center, two guards, and two offensive tackles) One quarterback One running back One tight end Three wide receivers
  • The defense's job is to prevent the offense from scoring points and they do this by tackling players who are carrying the football and forcing them to either commit a turnover (a fumble, interception, or turnover on downs) or punt the ball away. This includes Defensive linemen Linebackers Cornerbacks Safeties
  • Special teams are responsible for the kicking game.
  • When the offense starts with the ball, they will be on first down, and they will need to gain at least 10 yards over the next four plays. If they don't, the defensive team will have successfully made a stop, and their offense will take over at that point on the field. If the offense is able to gain those 10 yards, they will get a new set of "downs" to keep trying to proceed down the field.
